Our platform operates on a virtual credits or "tokens" system.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

1. Data We Collect

By registering at Romet Jewelry, we securely store the following information necessary to provide the service:

  • E-mail, First Name, and Last Name.
  • A history of generated designs (including Prompts and requested variables) encrypted and bound to your User ID.
  • Reference images you voluntarily upload through the "Upload your sketch" feature to our servers.

2. Use of Information

The collected information will be exclusively used to:

  • Identify you within the platform (Login/OAuth integrations).
  • Allocate and deduct your credits balance associated with your Cryptographic Identity (JWT).
  • Generate commercial manufacturing quotes and notify you via email about the status of your AI designs.

3. Protection and Cybersecurity

Password storage and access management are handled by world-class international providers (Supabase, Google, Microsoft), exempting Romet Jewelry from ever storing plain text passwords. Additionally, since April 2026, all API requests require strict encrypted JWT validation to prevent leaks or unwanted access (Row Level Security enforced).

4. GDPR & Data Rights

Every user possesses the right to Access, Rectify, Cancel, or Oppose their data records. If you wish to permanently purge all your activity and generated images from our cloud Databases and Buckets, please contact us and we will execute a complete data destruction within less than 48 hours.
